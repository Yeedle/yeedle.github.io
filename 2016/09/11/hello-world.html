Hello World

Published on: Sunday, September 11, 2016

Every book needs a cover, every jar a lid, and every blog a ‘hello world’ post. This is mine. Hello and welcome to my blog.

What kind of posts can you expect to see here? Well, for one, I’m a big fan of R, or more precisely, the #tidyverse. As Hadley Wickham, the high priest of the tidyverse himself, said

If you want to give back, I think writing a blog is a great way. Many of the things that you struggle with will be common problems. Think about how to solve them well and describe your solution to others.

So, a lot of what I hope to be doing here is “describe [my] solution to others.” I also hope to write about computer science/programming stuff in general. Finally, I hope to write posts to accompany some of my past and future projects.

Some blog-related credits: This blog is hosted on GitHub Pages, powered by Jekyll, themed by Lanyon, and generated by knitr[1] and is powered by VuePress.

  1. And you can too! On April 15, 2018 VueJS creator Evan You announced the release of VuePress, and I decided to switch to that. While VuePress is really geared towards generating documentation websites, having the power of Vue at my fingertips was really enticing, and converting to a blog was relatively easy.
